MelTown Music Overview

MelTown Music is a cultural IP rooted in the Chinese international student community in Oceania, dedicated to discovering and supporting Chinese original music and art. It promotes original music and artistic trends through diverse entertainment content, including audio-visual channels and live events across multiple music genres. MelTown Music aims to create a rich cultural brand with a sense of belonging for international students by offering a platform for diverse artistic expression.

Live Events

The live segment focuses on delivering high-quality live music events, featuring talented musicians and hosting competitive showcases.

Content Production

The content segment creates a variety of online content, such as short videos, variety shows, dramas, and podcasts, centered on the international student experience and original artistic elements. These efforts help promote the IP brand while supporting the live events through cross-platform engagement and original music promotion.

By integrating both online and offline elements, MelTown Music enriches the cultural life of the international student community with engaging content and events.
